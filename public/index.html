Our Mission

Our objectives for every student is to produce solid results and a fundamental knowledge of the coding language. The principles of computer science are stressed repeatedly throughout each course and we make sure to build student's confidence. We accommodate different learning styles and make sure that every student's pace is accounted for.

In addition to logic and problem-solving skills, we emphasize modern learning techniques that are made easy and understandable for a younger audience with easily accessable tools.

In addition to the services we provide to our students, Codeology, in close association with Saving By Coding, donates 100% of the profits to a selected charity in India that furthers educational opportunities for underprivileged kids. Saving By Coding is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that helps fund education in Indian schools that lack many facilities.

Our Impact






100% of our profits are donated to charities involved in furthering the educational opportunities available to young underprivileged kids across the world.


Saving by Coding is Codeology's parent organization. It is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that was created to inspire and support the underprivileged youth. Today's world is vastly developing, with innovations constantly on the rise. One major contributor for this constant development of innovations, is the impact of efficient education on todays youth. Many people in today's generation take for granted the education and guidance they receive, however this is not apparent all over the globe. In India, there are major cities and towns with a lack of proper support to provide a meaningful form of education. Saving By Coding directly donates to an Indian charity, Asha for Education.
