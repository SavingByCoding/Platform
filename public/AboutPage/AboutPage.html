ABOUT US





We hope to help our community by teaching students coding.




Codeology is an educational platform built by students for students. Codeology was created with the hope that through the platform, live calls, and curriculum, we could help students everywhere learn to code. Taking what we know, we have tried to make an environment cohesive to learning in a way that promotes retention and motivation for further learning. In the use of technology, there infinite possibilities in what can be done. We wish to open the eyes of students to those possibilities and give them the technical knowledge to pursue them.








As we look ahead into the next century, leaders will be those who empower others.

- Bill Gates








History


This organization was started two years ago as a mere idea to help others while pursuing a passion that we loved. Through painstaking work and efforts, over thousands of dollars have been raised and sent overseas and dozens of students taught in the ways of coding. As we continue our efforts, we plan to grow this into something that can be continued for years to come. Our goal is to never be satisfied with where we are. As a company, we plan to grow this into something that nurtures learning while also having fun in coding.
