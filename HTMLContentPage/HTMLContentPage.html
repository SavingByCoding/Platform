HTML Course Overview


Units
Lessons
Overview


Structure

Students will familiarize themselves with the building blocks of HTML - Elements, Tags, and Attributes.



Text

Students will learn how to present their text in different formats, using tags to make their content visually impressive.



Images

Students will learn how to use images appropriately to enhance their website.



Links

This lesson will go over how to link your website to pages all across the internet.



Lists

This lesson teaches students how to organize their content through different types of lists.



Tables

Students will learn how to properly implement and format tables to display content.



Forms

This lesson will review how to take in different forms of input from visitors through various ways.



Colors

Students will learn how to display text with color - all without the use of CSS.



Essential Additions

This lesson will go over the essential tags that every HTML programmer needs to include within their code.



Flash, Video, and Audio

This lesson will teach students how to effectively implement videos and audios across their website.
