ABOUT US





We are a group of alike-minded high school students dedicated to aiding our community and the world at large. We wish to provide kids a platform to learn, engage, and excel with the world of coding.



Mission


Saving by Coding is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that was created to inspire and support the underprivileged youth. Today's world is vastly developing, with innovations constantly on the rise. One major contributor for this constant development of innovations, is the impact of efficient education on todays youth. Many people in today's generation take for granted the education and guidance they receive, however this is not apparent all over the globe. In India, there are major cities and towns with a lack of proper support to provide a meaningful form of education. Saving By Coding directly donates to an Indian charity, Asha for Education, and this helps schools like Shanmugananda Elementary School support the education of their students.

History


This organization was started two years ago as a mere idea to help others while pursuing a passion that we loved. Through painstaking work and efforts, over thousands of dollars have been raised and sent overseas. As just high school students, this is a big deal for us but we plan to grow this into something that can be continued for years to come. Our goal is to never be satisfied with where we are. As a company, we plan to grow this into something that nurtures happiness and growth among kids that have never experience happiness or growth from education.
